The 200-Year-Old Whisper

Step inside and feel the temperature drop as the 200-year-old thick lime-washed walls breathe. Our story isn't just about food; it's about the seven decades of sailors, dreamers, and locals who have shared conversations over these very wooden tables.

Founded in 1955 by the ancestors of the current family, Venite remains a sanctuary of Indo-Portuguese hospitality in the heart of Panjim. Every crack in the plaster tells a story of a Goa that was.
